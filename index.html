About the Book

From alleyways that whisper to crows that wear shades, each story in this collection invites you into a world where the lines between reality and the subconscious blur. When Crows Wore Sunglasses isn’t just a book—it’s a mood, a shadow, and sometimes, a confession.

Written with cinematic precision and literary depth, these stories explore modern alienation, guilt-ridden decisions, and mythic symbolism through gripping characters and surreal narratives.
